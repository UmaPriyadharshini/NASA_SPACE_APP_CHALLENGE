Welcome to Zephyrion !.
In designing an imaginary planet, we envision a world meticulously crafted for habitability in extreme conditions. While shelter remains crucial for survival, this planet's environment is uniquely forgiving, allowing individuals to endure without it for a limited time. Abundant, life-sustaining air graces this world, granting individuals up to two precious minutes of respite without it. Furthermore, the planet boasts a generous 50% water content, guaranteeing a perpetual source of hydration and affording individuals at least a full day of existence without this essential element.
